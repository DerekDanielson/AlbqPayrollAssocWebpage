The Albuquerque APA Chapter proudly supports the following local charities



Casa Esperanza is a “home away from home” for cancer patients and their families. The Chapter holds occasional dinners for the current residents, as well as raising much-needed donations at our lunch meetings and other events.



Domestic Violence Resource Center is our second charity and their focus is to make a positive impact in the lives of adult and child victims in order to break the cycle of domestic abuse and violence. We support them through contributions and attending their fundraisers. To learn more about the Domestic Violence Resource Center of New Mexico.
