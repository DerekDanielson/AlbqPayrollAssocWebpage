Welcome to the Albuquerque Chapter of the American Payroll Association (ABQAPA), we are affiliated and accredited by the National American Payroll Association. This site has been created to provide payroll professionals and their colleagues up to date information about what the ABQAPA is doing to enhance the payroll community, as well as keep you up to date on all events.

The Albuquerque APA is always looking for new members. Anyone involved in payroll, human resources, compensation, benefits, and accounting fields is eligible to apply. We would be happy to have you learn more about our group of payroll professionals.
