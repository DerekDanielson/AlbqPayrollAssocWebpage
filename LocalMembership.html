Local Membership

Benefits of becoming a member of the Albuquerque APA Chapter:

  • Chapter meetings are an excellent source of information and exchange between payroll professionals.
  • Advance your career as a payroll professional. ABQAPA offers you the opportunity to join a study group that will prepare you for taking either the Certified Payroll Professional (CPP) or Fundamentals of Payroll Compliance (FPC) exam from the American Payroll Association. These study groups meet weekly and are led by qualified CPPs.
  • A scholarship is awarded annually to a member desiring to become a CPP/FPC. The scholarship covers the study group cost, books and the exam fee after passing the test.
  • Contribute to the profession and to the community! The Albuquerque APA encourages its members to get involved.
  • Reduced rates for luncheons
  • Members only communications
  • Certification classes
  • RCH Credits
